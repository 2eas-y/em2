How It Works

Your Journey with Emreede

01

Contact Us

Book a free study guidance session or start an application. We’ll get back to you within 48 hours and guide you forward.

02

You Get a Personal Coach

We help you find schools that match your goals and preferences. Our advice is neutral and independent.

03

Application to Universities

You get direct contact with your chosen university and fast, reliable, personalized support from your advisor.

04

Admission Decision

Once you receive an offer, you can choose to accept or decline. Your application is never binding.

05

Student Financing Guidance

We help you understand your funding options and what support is available for your studies abroad.

06

Scholarships

We offer exclusive scholarships for many universities and assist with additional scholarship opportunities for international students.

07

Housing Abroad

After being accepted, you can apply for student housing through the university or find accommodation on your own. We provide plenty of tips and guidance.

08

Visa Support

For studies outside the EU, a student visa is usually required. We help with instructions, advice, and step-by-step guidance.

09

Connect with Other Students

We connect you with other students heading to the same destination and with students already there.

10

Insurance

It’s important to have the right insurance when studying abroad. We advise you on insurance options and work with major providers.
