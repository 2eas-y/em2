Application Timeline

Am I On Time?

Understanding the application cycle is key to success.

Early

12-24 Months Before

Ideal for top-tier universities. Plenty of time for profile building, exam prep, and summer schools.

On Time

9-12 Months Before

Standard application window. Focus on essays, finalizing lists, and submitting applications.

Late

Less than 6 Months

Limited options but still possible. Requires urgent action and flexible university choices.
